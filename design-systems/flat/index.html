Design loud, edit ruthlessly

Flat design gets called simple, but it's the opposite of easy. With no shadows or gradients to hide behind, every decision is visible — spacing, color, and type carry the whole interface.

RuleIf removing an element changes nothing, it was decoration. Remove it.

That's the whole system: a few honest colors, thick lines, and the confidence to leave everything else out.
