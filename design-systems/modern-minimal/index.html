Why we throttle our own release notes

Every feature we ship competes for the same resource: your attention. So we made a rule — one announcement per week, no matter how much we shipped. The rest lands quietly in the changelog.

Note — this policy applies to emails too. You will never get two in one week.

It turns out restraint compounds. Open rates doubled, and support tickets about "what changed?" fell by a third.
