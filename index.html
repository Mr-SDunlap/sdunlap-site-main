Who Am I?

Stephen Dunlap
Front-End Designer
He - Him - His
I’m someone who likes building things: ideas, interfaces, meals, and the occasional 3D-printed object. I enjoy reading, cooking, biking, photography, philosophy, and any kind of creative process that lets me explore structure and form. I earned my BA in Architecture & Innovative Design in 2019, which shaped how I think about systems, space, and intentional design. Since then, I’ve migrated into the world of digital design and computer science, drawn to the overlap of planning, structure, and building in both physical and virtual spaces.

What I Do

I design and build web experiences that focus on usability, accessibility, and real-world implementation. My work sits between UX, visual design, and front-end execution, helping turn complex requirements into clear, usable interfaces.

UX & Planning

I start by understanding the problem, the user’s context, and the constraints of the system before moving into design.

My UX process focuses on:

  • clarifying requirements and goals
  • supporting user flows and information hierarchy
  • collaborating with UX partners on testing and validation
  • aligning stakeholders early to reduce confusion

I don’t run user testing directly, but I work closely with UX teams to iterate on designs based on feedback and real-world use.

Design & Creative Direction

Once structure is in place, I translate wireframes into finished designs that reflect both brand identity and user intent.

This includes:

  • applying visual systems and creative direction
  • designing layouts, typography, and interface elements
  • creating supporting art and visual assets
  • thinking through motion and interaction as part of the experience

I try to keep clarity at the center of everything. Visual design should support usability, not compete with it.

Front-End & Implementation

As a Front-End Designer, I focus on bridging design and development.

I'm comfortable working with:

  • HTML, CSS/SASS, and JavaScript fundamentals
  • no-code and low-code platforms for interactive experiences
  • collaborating with engineers on production builds
  • launching sites with accessibility, performance, and SEO in mind

I design with implementation in mind so ideas translate cleanly into real, maintainable experiences.
