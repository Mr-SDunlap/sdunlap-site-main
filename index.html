Systems Thinking

Before anything gets designed or built, I map the components that need to operate together — and the edges that will stress at scale. Every engagement starts with understanding the structure, not the surface.

User research and A/B testing inform the decisions. Modular design systems make them scalable. Every component is intentional, every pattern reusable — built to stay cohesive as the product grows.
